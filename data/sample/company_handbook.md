# Welcome to Nexora

Welcome to Nexora Technologies! We're excited to have you on the team. This handbook covers everything you need to know about working at Nexora, from our culture and values to practical policies and benefits.

## Our Mission

We build intelligent infrastructure for the next generation of AI-powered applications. Our mission is to make AI systems more reliable, maintainable, and production-ready.

## Our Values

- **Reliability First**: We build systems that work when you need them most
- **Transparent Engineering**: Every decision is documented and explainable
- **Continuous Learning**: We invest in our team's growth and development
- **Customer Obsession**: Our users' success drives every product decision

# Employment Policies

## Work Arrangements

Nexora supports flexible work arrangements. All employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Team leads coordinate in-office schedules to ensure adequate collaboration time.

### Office Locations

Our main offices are located in:
- San Francisco, CA (Headquarters)
- Austin, TX (Engineering Hub)
- London, UK (European Operations)

### Remote Work Guidelines

Remote employees must:
1. Maintain a dedicated workspace with reliable internet
2. Be available during core hours (10am-3pm in their local timezone)
3. Attend all scheduled team meetings
4. Use company-approved security tools and VPN

## Time Off

### Paid Time Off (PTO)

All full-time employees receive:
- 20 days PTO per year (accrued monthly)
- 5 additional sick days
- 10 company holidays
- 2 floating holidays

PTO requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Emergency time off is handled on a case-by-case basis.

### Parental Leave

We offer 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for all new parents, regardless of gender. This applies to birth, adoption, and foster placement.

### Sabbatical

After 5 years of continuous employment, employees are eligible for a 4-week paid sabbatical. After 10 years, this extends to 8 weeks.

# Compensation and Benefits

## Salary Structure

Salaries are reviewed annually in Q1. Our compensation philosophy is to pay in the top quartile for each role and location. Salary bands are transparent and published in our internal wiki.

## Equity

All employees receive stock options as part of their compensation package. Options vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. We conduct quarterly board meetings to approve any option grants.

## Health Insurance

Nexora provides comprehensive health coverage:
- Medical (PPO and HMO options)
- Dental
- Vision
- Mental health and counseling services
- Health Savings Account with company contribution

### Wellness Stipend

Each employee receives a $1,200 annual wellness stipend for gym memberships, fitness equipment, wellness apps, or other health-related expenses.

## Retirement

We offer a 401(k) plan with 4% company match, vested immediately. Employees can contribute up to the IRS annual limit.

# Professional Development

## Learning Budget

Every employee has an annual $3,000 learning budget for conferences, courses, books, and certifications. Requests are approved by your manager through the L&D portal.

## Internal Mobility

We encourage internal transfers and career changes. Employees who have been in their current role for at least 12 months can apply for open positions in other teams.

## Mentorship Program

New hires are paired with a senior mentor for their first 6 months. The mentorship program also supports cross-functional learning and leadership development.

# Code of Conduct

## Respect and Inclusion

We are committed to creating an inclusive workplace where everyone feels valued. Discrimination, harassment, and bullying are not tolerated under any circumstances.

## Reporting Concerns

If you experience or witness inappropriate behavior, report it through:
1. Your direct manager
2. HR portal (anonymous option available)
3. Ethics hotline: 1-800-NEXORA-1

All reports are investigated promptly and confidentially.

## Intellectual Property

All work created during employment belongs to Nexora Technologies. Open source contributions must be approved by the Engineering Lead and Legal team.

# Security and Compliance

## Data Security

All employees must:
- Use two-factor authentication on all company systems
- Complete annual security training
- Report any suspected security incidents immediately
- Follow the clean desk policy when working in the office

## Customer Data

We take customer data privacy seriously. All customer data must be handled according to our Data Classification Policy. Access to production data requires manager approval and is logged.

## Incident Response

In case of a security incident:
1. Contact the Security team immediately via #security-incident Slack channel
2. Do not attempt to fix the issue yourself
3. Document what you observed
4. Wait for instructions from the Incident Commander

# IT and Equipment

## Company Equipment

New employees receive:
- MacBook Pro (M-series chip)
- External monitor (27" 4K)
- Keyboard and mouse
- Headset for video calls

## Software

All employees get licenses for:
- IDE of choice (JetBrains suite available)
- Design tools (Figma)

## Expense Policy

Business expenses under $100 require manager approval. Expenses over $100 require VP approval. Submit all expenses within 30 days through the expense management system.
