Corporate Policy Overview

This document outlines the corporate policies for Nexora Technologies. All employees, contractors, and third-party partners are required to adhere to these policies.

These policies are reviewed quarterly and updated as needed. The most current version is always available on the internal wiki.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Nexora collects only the minimum data necessary to provide our services. We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account information (name, email, company)
  • Usage data (API calls, processing volume, error rates)
  • Document metadata (file types, processing timestamps)
  • Support interaction data (tickets, chat logs)

Data Processing

All data is processed in accordance with GDPR and CCPA regulations. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Processing is performed in the region where the customer's account is registered. We maintain data residency in the US, EU, and APAC regions.

Data Subject Rights

Customers and end users have the right to:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of their data
  • Export their data in a portable format
  • Object to processing of their data

Acceptable Use Policy

Permitted Uses

The DocFlow platform may be used for:

  • Processing documents for legitimate business purposes
  • Building internal knowledge management systems
  • Creating AI-powered search and retrieval applications
  • Research and development of document processing techniques

Prohibited Uses

The following uses are strictly prohibited:

  • Processing illegally obtained documents
  • Creating deepfakes or misleading content
  • Surveillance or unauthorized monitoring
  • Any activity that violates applicable laws
  • Attempting to reverse engineer the platform

Data Retention Policy

Retention Periods

Data is retained according to the following schedule:

  • Active documents: Retained for the duration of the subscription
  • Deleted documents: Purged within 30 days of deletion
  • Processing logs: Retained for 90 days
  • Audit logs: Retained for 1 year
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)

Data Backup

We maintain encrypted backups with the following characteristics:

  • Incremental backups every 6 hours
  • Full backups daily
  • Backups retained for 30 days
  • Geographically distributed storage

Security Policy

Infrastructure Security

All infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2 Type II certified cloud providers. Network access is restricted through VPCs, security groups, and VPN tunnels.

Application Security

We follow OWASP best practices for application security:

  • All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Regular penetration testing by third-party firms
  • Automated vulnerability scanning in CI/CD pipeline
  • Bug bounty program for responsible disclosure

Access Control

Access to systems follows the principle of least privilege:

  • Role-based access control (RBAC) for all systems
  • Mandatory two-factor authentication
  • Access reviews conducted quarterly
  • Privileged access requires manager approval and is time-limited

Incident Response

Security incidents are handled according to our Incident Response Plan:

  • Detection and triage within 15 minutes
  • Initial assessment within 1 hour
  • Customer notification within 24 hours (if data is affected)
  • Post-incident review within 5 business days
